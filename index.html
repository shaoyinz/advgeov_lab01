Introduction

A map based on NPP-VIIRS night light data to show the economic condition in the Michigan.

Data Source

The Visible Infrared Imaging Radiometer Suite (VIIRS) is one of the key instruments onboard the Suomi National Polar-Orbiting Partnership (Suomi NPP) spacecraft, which was successfully launched on October 28, 2011. https://ncc.nesdis.noaa.gov/VIIRS/

Earth Observation Group had released a new consistently processed time series of annual global VIIRS nighttime lights from monthly cloud-free average radiance grids spanning 2012* to 2021. That is called VNL V2. https://eogdata.mines.edu/nighttime_light/annual/v20

Data processing

Grab data

Clicking on the above link, a list of annual average nighttime light will shown. To show a static economic condition based on VIIRS, we only need the image from one year. Let’s download data from 2021, and chose the average night light image.
